Software Engineering

I always loved computers from a young age. At 14, I taught myself to code in C++, and soon after enrolled in my high school Computer Science classes. I knew then that this was what I wanted to do for the rest of my life. I went on to earn a Bachelor's degree in Software Engineering from the University of Waterloo in 2018 and have been working at Block (formerly known as Square) since then. In March 2025, I obtained my professional engineering licence through Professional Engineers Ontario (PEO).

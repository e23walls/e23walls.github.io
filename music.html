Music

Music has been a part of my life for as long as I can remember. I studied piano growing up, but it was singing that truly moved me. After some casual performances over the years, I began classical voice training as a hobby in 2018 and have continued ever since.

About My Voice

I am a soprano with a deep love for French Romantic art song. Fauré's mélodies are my particular favourites, though I find myself drawn to the repertoire broadly.

Performances

In 2019, I performed at Marymount College in a recital with the New York Conservatory of Music, where I sang "Clair de Lune" by Gabriel Fauré. Over the years I have also performed in smaller venues (churches and the like) and I never miss an opportunity for karaoke.

Recordings

I have not done much recording in recent years. This is something I am hoping to change! My recordings are a mix of classical songs and showtunes.
